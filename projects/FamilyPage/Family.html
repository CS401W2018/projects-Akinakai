The Family Members

Takashi Hamada

The oldest of the brothers. The first to enter college and the creator of Baymax. He died leaving Baymax and the will to help others with his younger brother Hiro Hamada. Takashi wanted to build a robot that could revolutionizing the medical industry. Before he passed he had completed his life's goal with Baymax.

Hiro Hamada

The youngest of the brothers, he graduated high school at 13 and became an underground bot fighter in downtown Tokyo. His intelligence helped him win many fights, allowing him to earn a substantial amount of money. Inspired by the dedication and hard work of Takashi's classmates, he wanted to go to college like his brother to help create a better society. After Takashi's death, he inherited Baymax.

Baymax

The Oldest Brother creatation and the newest addition to the Hamada Family. Baymax Has a AI generitve learning system to help with the medical health care. Baymax was built to help with first reasponders in the hope to increase chance of survival. Baymax later learns marial arts from the program given to him by Hiro to help avenge his brothers death. Baymax and Hiro start the super hero group later knowm as Big Hero 6.
